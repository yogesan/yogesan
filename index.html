About Me

Hi! I'm Yogesan, a passionate researcher focused on exploring microbial evolution and antimicrobial resistance. My goal is to understand the unknown facts of life and contribute to scientific progress. Currently, I am working on several exciting research projects related to microbiology and biochemistry.
